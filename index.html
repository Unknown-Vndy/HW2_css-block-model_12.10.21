Bicycles

Did you know that more than 100 million bicycles are manufactured each year? And that there are over 1 billion bicycles that are currently being used and ridden all around the world?

But did you ever wonder how bicycles really came into existence?

Bicycles have quite a varied history that goes back about more than 600 years, during which the bicycle was extremely different than the one you know of today.

They have significantly evolved over time with major innovations that dramatically transformed all of its form and features.

Road bike

They are designed to be ridden fast on smooth pavement. most people find them uncomfortable and unstable on unpaved trails.

Advanture bike

They are sometimes called all-road bikes, any-road bikes, or gravel bikes, and are the most versatile sub-category of road bike.

Mountain bike

They are design for riding rough off-road trails. They have flat or upright handlebars, and a very low gear range for pedaling up steep trails.

Maintenance

Basic maintenance item is regular lubrication of the chain and pivot points for derailleurs and brakes.
